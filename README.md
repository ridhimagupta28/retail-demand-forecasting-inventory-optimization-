## Retail demand forecasting & inventory optimization

An end-to-end Machine learning and Business intelligence project that analyzes historical Walmart sales data to forecast product demand and generate inventory optimization insights.

This project combines **SQL**, **Python**, **Machine learning**, and **Power BI** to help retailers make data-driven decisions regarding inventory management, pricing, and sales forecasting.

---

## 📌 Project overview

Retail businesses often struggle with balancing inventory levels. Overstocking increases holding costs, while understocking leads to lost sales and dissatisfied customers.

This project addresses these challenges by:

- Forecasting future product demand
- Identifying top and low-performing products
- Analyzing sales trends and seasonality
- Measuring the impact of pricing on sales
- Creating an interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights

---

## 🎯 Objectives

## 📈 Machine Learning Models

### XGBoost

Used for demand forecasting by learning complex relationships between historical sales, pricing, seasonality, and calendar events.

### ARIMA

Used as a statistical baseline time-series forecasting model for comparison with XGBoost.
